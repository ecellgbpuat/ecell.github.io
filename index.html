Who We Are

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of GBPUA&T Pantnagar is a student run organisation working under the guidance of Directorate Placement And Counseling. Ecell Pantnagar was established with objective of creating, fostering and promoting spirit of Entrepreneurship amongst the youth of the nation.

Our Vision

To keep the candle of entrepreneurship lighting among the youth, to encourage, enhance, equip and promote the budding Entrepreneurs of India.

Our Initiatives

Events like catalyst and The annual business festival Footprints, students with innovative ideas are selected and guided on how their idea could be realised.

Our Achievements

Within one year of E-Cell's establishment we have successfully setup incubation centre at Pantnagar
